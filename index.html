Project 2: Scalable Personal Icon

For this project, I need to develop a suite of three icons that represent myself. The three personal icons I designed are Majhong, my right hand plamar crease and Zen buddhism. My design concept is to make the three icons look unified and simple. Majhong is a fun game, originated in China. All my family members play Mahjong during the festival, So influenced by them, it has become my favorite board game.
